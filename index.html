Our Mission

Our goal at TSafe is to give people the information they need to make an informed choice about their daily commute, as well as the tools and resources they require to move around Toronto safely. Whether someone is walking, riding, or using public transportation, we think everyone has the right to feel secure and safe as they go about their daily lives.

We hope to assist users in locating the safest paths to their destinations using our cutting-edge software, avoiding potentially hazardous places and high-risk circumstances. We are in a position to offer up-to-date information on crime hotspots and other safety concerns by collaborating with local law enforcement, utilising real-time data, and soliciting community comment

Safer Routes

The safe route functionality of TSafe is a feature designed to help users navigate from one location to another while prioritizing safety. This feature takes into account various factors such as crime rates, pedestrian safety, and the availability of well-lit areas to generate a route that is less likely to expose the user to potential danger.

Real Time Updates

The real-time crime updates functionality TSafe is a feature designed to keep users informed about criminal activity happening in their area. This feature uses location-based data and updates in real-time to notify users of any crimes reported near their current location or in areas they have marked as important to them.

User Provided Incidents

The user-provided incidents functionality of TSafe is a feature that allows users to report and share information about incidents that they have witnessed or experienced. This feature enables users to contribute to a community-driven database of information about incidents that are relevant to their location or interests.

About The Team

A group of people who have firsthand experience with the perils of commuting in a high-crime city formed TSafe. They understood that there was a genuine need for a solution that could aid travellers in avoiding potential dangers and making them feel more comfortable. Given this, Tsafe set out to develop a safer route app that would enable users to prepare their routes in advance and steer clear of any potential risks or dangers.
